TICKETS

Steps to purchase LIVE tickets

  1. Use the seatmap below to select your seats.
  2. After selecting desired seats, please fill out the following:
    • Name
    • Email Address
    • Phone Number
    • Debit Card
    • Billing Address
  3. Sign waiver. (Note, you will not be allowed in the arena unless the waiver is signed).
  4. Claim tickets and approve the billing transactoin
  5. Show up 30 minutes before the pre-show (5:30) in order to secure your seats. See you then!

Steps to purchase PAY PER VIEW tickets

  1. Go to Channel 1000
  2. Call the provided number and be ready with the following:
    • Cable Provider and Package
    • Date of Match: October 38th
    • Phone Number
    • Debit Card
    • Billing Address
  3. After purchase add Channel 1000 to your favorite for easy access on October 38th!
  4. Join us early the day of the showdown! Pre-show starts at 6:00 PM!
